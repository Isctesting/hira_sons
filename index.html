About Hira Sons


Founded over five decades ago, we stand firm on the pillars of integrity, trust, and unwavering commitment to excellence. Our journey is not merely a testament to our longevity, but a reflection of the enduring relationships we have cultivated with our clients, partners, and communities.

Our name "Hira" Sons is derived from the Sanskrit word "hīraka," meaning "diamond," literally translates to ‘Diamond Sons or Sons of Diamonds’ highlighting our passion and commitment to the brilliance and timelessness associated with this precious gemstone.

Why Us?


For over half a century, we have remained steadfast in upholding the highest standards of craftsmanship in diamond cutting, diamond recycling and jewellery making.

Our craftsmen have perfected the art and science of processing closeout jewellery and closeout diamonds to deliver uncompromising quality and finish. We purchase closeout diamonds and closeout jewellery on a large scale and pride ourselves in quick turnarounds and favorable terms for our partners
